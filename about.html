Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leadership

Building Trust Since 2008

Founded in Johannesburg in 2008, Lukiya Group began as a small team of IT enthusiasts with a vision to transform how businesses leverage technology. What started as a data networking specialist has grown into a comprehensive IT solutions provider serving clients nationwide.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Over the years, we've expanded our service offerings to meet the evolving needs of our clients, always staying ahead of technological trends.

Today, we're proud to have completed over 850 projects for businesses ranging from startups to Fortune 500 companies, maintaining a 98% client satisfaction rate throughout our history.
